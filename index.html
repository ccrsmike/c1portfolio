My Experience

February 2003 - May 2012

I reported directly to the CEO and was responsible for hardware, software, and network monitoring, as well as the management of a Squid proxy running Red Hat Linux Enterprise. Additionally, I successfully installed and managed the Maze financial database across the entire network.
